VoxelCraft

A minimalist Minecraft‑style voxel engine with chunk‑based terrain, procedural biomes, and block interaction.

Features

  • Chunk‑based infinite terrain
  • Perlin‑noise heightmaps and simple biomes
  • Block placement and destruction
  • Texture‑atlas based rendering
  • First‑person movement (walk, jump, sprint)

How to Play

  • WASD – Move
  • Space – Jump
  • Shift – Sprint
  • Left Click – Break block
  • Right Click – Place block
